How to use Spin the Wheel

1

Add the Items

Add the items you want in the text box on the right, one per line. They can be names, numbers, phrases, or anything you like!

2

Change the Settings

Select the rotation duration (slow, normal, or fast) and decide whether to enable the elimination mode to automatically remove winners

3

Spin the Wheel

Click the central 'Spin' button to make the wheel spin. Wait for it to stop to see the result. With elimination mode enabled, the winner will be automatically removed

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I save my wheel configuration?

Currently, the wheel configuration is saved in your local browser. We're working on implementing an account system that will allow you to save and share your custom wheels.

Can I use this for live draws?

‚ñº

Absolutely! Spin the Wheel is perfect for live draws, both in person and online. You can share your screen during a video call or project the site during an event.

How many items can I add to the wheel?

‚ñº

You can add up to 50 items to the wheel. For better visualization, we recommend keeping the number of items between 2 and 20, but the system works with more items as well.

Is the wheel truly random?

‚ñº

Yes, Spin the Wheel uses a random number generator to determine the winner, ensuring that each item has the same probability of being selected.

Certainly! You can toggle the sound effects on or off at any time using the audio button in the bottom right corner of the screen.
